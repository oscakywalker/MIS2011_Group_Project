Below are the key website prototype features of SipPulse, presented to illustrate how the proposed system supports beverage enterprises in monitoring public opinion, identifying reputational risks, and translating customer feedback into practical strategic action. The interface has been designed to emphasize clarity, responsiveness, and managerial usability, ensuring that users can move efficiently from enterprise onboarding to insight generation within a single connected service environment.

The Homepage and Service Intake interface serves as the primary entry point to the SipPulse platform. Through this page, enterprise users can enter the name of the beverage brand requiring analysis, initiate the assessment process, and obtain a clear view of the system's end-to-end workflow. In addition to introducing the platform's value proposition, the homepage highlights the integrated social media sources and downstream analytical modules, thereby establishing a structured and professional first-touch experience for prospective clients.

The Account Access and Subscription Management interfaces support secure user onboarding and differentiated service access. The login module enables users to enter their workspace and manage account credentials in a streamlined manner, while the upgrade page presents subscription tiers with clear feature comparisons. Together, these interfaces reinforce the platform's commercial feasibility by demonstrating how SipPulse can accommodate both entry-level users and premium enterprise clients seeking broader analytical capabilities.

The Social Media Source Monitoring interfaces showcase SipPulse's ability to capture and organize consumer discussions from multiple high-relevance digital platforms, including Rednote, Douyin, Weibo, and Dianping. Each module is tailored to the communication characteristics of its source platform, allowing the system to surface sentiment-rich comments, recurring complaints, and emerging discussion patterns with greater contextual precision. This multi-source architecture strengthens the platform's monitoring value by providing enterprises with a more comprehensive and balanced view of brand perception across the digital ecosystem.

The Sentiment Analysis interface translates large volumes of unstructured comments into actionable emotional intelligence. Through filtering conditions, sentiment categorization, and segmentation outputs, the module enables users to identify prevailing consumer attitudes and isolate negative opinion clusters efficiently. By converting dispersed public commentary into interpretable sentiment insights, this feature supports faster managerial diagnosis and allows beverage brands to respond to customer concerns with greater accuracy and confidence.

The Risk Monitoring Visualization interface enables users to identify abnormal fluctuations in sentiment performance and detect high-priority reputational threats in a timely manner. By presenting source concentration, emotional distribution, and trend-based warning signals through an accessible visual dashboard, the module strengthens managerial awareness of emerging public opinion risks. This feature is particularly valuable for beverage enterprises that require rapid escalation pathways when negative commentary begins to intensify across multiple platforms.

The Emotional Trend Prediction interface extends SipPulse beyond static monitoring by introducing a forward-looking analytical perspective. Drawing on historical sentiment patterns, the module projects likely emotional trajectories and enables managers to assess whether overall brand perception is improving, stabilizing, or deteriorating over time. This predictive capability supports earlier intervention and strengthens the platform's value for enterprises seeking not only to interpret current sentiment, but also to anticipate future reputational developments.

The AI-Generated Solutions interface transforms analytical outputs into practical managerial recommendations. Based on the sentiment, risk, and trend findings produced by earlier modules, the system generates targeted improvement suggestions, response strategies, and actionable communication drafts that can support internal decision-making and external brand response. By linking insight generation directly to strategic action, this feature reinforces SipPulse's role as a proactive brand management platform rather than a purely observational system.
